# Communication Plan

## Channels

| Channel | Tool | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Project tracking | Jira Free | Sprint board, tasks, ownership, progress |
| Knowledge base | Confluence Free | Decisions, research notes, meeting notes |
| Code | GitHub Free | Version control, pull requests, issues |
| Fast chat | WhatsApp or Slack Free | Urgent coordination and quick decisions |
| Meetings | Google Meet | Planning, reviews, unblock sessions |

## Recommended Weekly Schedule

| Meeting | Time | Duration | Owner | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sprint Planning | Saturday 9:30 PM IST / 12:00 PM ET | 45 min | Rohit | Select sprint goals and tickets |
| Async Daily Standup | Daily before 11:00 AM IST | 5 min/person | Everyone | Progress, plan, blockers |
| Midweek Sync | Wednesday 9:30 PM IST / 12:00 PM ET | 30 min | Rohit | Resolve blockers and review risks |
| Sprint Review + Retro | Friday 9:30 PM IST / 12:00 PM ET | 45 min | Everyone | Demo progress and improve process |
