# Sprint Planning Agenda

## Purpose

Select a realistic sprint goal and assign work that moves the team toward hackathon submission readiness.

## Agenda

1. Review upcoming hackathon deadline.
2. Review previous sprint carryover.
3. Confirm sprint goal.
4. Pull highest-priority tickets into the sprint.
5. Assign owners and story points.
6. Identify risks and blockers.
7. Confirm demo expectations for sprint review.

## Output

- Sprint goal written in Jira
- Tickets assigned
- Blockers recorded
- Meeting notes posted to Confluence
