# Leadership Narrative

## Core Story

CourtVision AI predicts Wimbledon outcomes by combining tennis domain signals with explainable machine learning. The team focuses on reproducibility, validation, and clear communication, not just raw predictions.

## Presentation Arc

1. **Problem:** Wimbledon predictions are uncertain because rankings alone miss surface fit, recent form, draw difficulty, and matchup dynamics.
2. **Approach:** Build a structured data pipeline, baseline models, stronger ML models, and explainable outputs.
3. **Validation:** Backtest against prior Wimbledon tournaments and compare against ranking-only baselines.
4. **Prediction:** Show top 8 and knockout-stage outputs with confidence and risk.
5. **Insight:** Explain what the model learned about grass performance, form, and player volatility.
6. **Execution:** Demonstrate that the team operated like a real analytics product team.

## Winning Angle

The project should feel credible, not overcomplicated. The strongest message is:

> We built a repeatable decision system that improves on simple rankings, explains its reasoning, and adapts as the tournament progresses.
